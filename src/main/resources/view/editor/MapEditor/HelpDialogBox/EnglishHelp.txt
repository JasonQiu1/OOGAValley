This is the Map Editor! You can use it to edit the
game map.



-You can use the \"Change Grid Size\" button
to set the grid to a specific size

-To add or remove a row or column from a specific
side, click the box that appears when your mouse
hovers over edges of the grid
(Click Green to Grow and Red to Shrink)

-Select Tiles from the Selector at the bottom of the
Map Editor and then click a grid cell to place the tile


-Once a tile is placed, you can place nature, plants,
and buildings on the tile

-To remove tiles, nature, plants, and buildings from a
cell, right click cell

-Running your mouse over a cell will display that cell's
location and any selectables placed in the cell

Have Fun!
